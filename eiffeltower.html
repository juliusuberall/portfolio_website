EIFFEL TOWER
The Eiffel Tower is a 324-meter tall iron lattice tower located in Paris, France. Designed by Gustave Eiffel and built in 1889 for the Exposition Universelle, it is considered a masterpiece of engineering and an iconic example of 19th-century French architecture.

As part of my first year undergraduate studies in architecture and interior design in Germany, I remodelled the Eiffel Tower in CAD following historic architectural drawings.
