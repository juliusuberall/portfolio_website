NICE TO MEET YOU.
I am Julius Noah Benjamin Überall, a computational designer and software developer currently based in London, United Kingdom. Cross connecting disciplines to create value and innovation for a better future of humanity is what drives me. My major interests lie within the domain of computer science, design, and three-dimensionality.

Born and raised in Germany, I studied in Germany, the United States, and the United Kingdom. I received my Abitur in 2017 from the Quirinus-Gymnasium Neuss in Germany. I graduated in 2021, with the dean's recognition for best in year, with a Bachelor of Arts in architecture and interior design from the University of Applied Sciences in Düsseldorf, Germany. I studied abroad in 2019 at Virginia Tech in the United States, majoring in architecture. In September 2022, I will fulfill my Master of Science in Architectural Computation at the Bartlett School of Architecture (University College London) in London, United Kingdom. Since 2020 I am a scholarship holder at the German Academic Scholarship Foundation.

Until today I spent most of my available time as one of the Co-Founders of the jewelry brand YŪ, which I founded in 2019 with my dean's research assistant from the University of Applied Sciences Düsseldorf. Furthermore, I worked as a student assistant at the University of Applied Sciences Düsseldorf during my undergraduate, whereby I supported Dean Prof. Dipl. -Ing. Judith Reitz, Prof. Dipl. -Ing. Moritz Fleischmann and Prof. Dr. -Ing. Eike Musall.

Currently, I am seeking challenging, inspiring, and professional environments to grow and research in to shape the future. I am eager to learn new techniques, gain perspectives and catch up with our fast-paced blue little planet.
