NICE TO MEET YOU.
I am Julius Noah Benjamin Überall, a computational designer and software developer currently based in London, United Kingdom. Cross connecting disciplines to create value and innovation for a better future of humanity is what drives me. My major interests lie within the domain of computer science, design, and complex geometry.

Born and raised in Germany, I studied in Germany, the United States, and the United Kingdom. I received my Abitur (high-school diploma) in 2017 from the Quirinus-Gymnasium Neuss in Germany. I graduated in 2021, with the dean's recognition for best in year, with a Bachelor of Arts in architecture and interior design from the University of Applied Sciences in Dusseldorf, Germany. I studied abroad in 2019 at Virginia Tech in the United States, majoring in architecture. In September 2022, I graduated with a Master of Science in Architectural Computation at the Bartlett School of Architecture (UCL) in London, United Kingdom. From 2020 until 2022 I was a scholarship holder at the German Academic Scholarship Foundation.

Since October 2022 I am working in the Applied Research + Development department (ARD) at Foster and Partners in London.

Until today I spent most of my time as one of the Co-Founders of the jewelry brand YŪ, which I founded in 2019 with my dean's research assistant from the University of Applied Sciences Düsseldorf. Furthermore, I worked as a student assistant at the University of Applied Sciences Düsseldorf during my undergraduate, whereby I supported Dean Prof. Dipl. -Ing. Judith Reitz, Prof. Dipl. -Ing. Moritz Fleischmann and Prof. Dr. -Ing. Eike Musall.
