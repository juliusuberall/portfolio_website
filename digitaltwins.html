DIGITAL TWINS
Digital twins capture real-world objects, including furniture, humans and architecture. These virtual replicas are used across digital systems.
The presented collection is digitally capturing real-world architecture and motivated by pure interest into 3D scanning technology. The technology stack consists out of Unity and Vuforia, which is an augemented reality software development kit for mobile devices, enabling 3D scanning with the iPhone's LIDAR sensor.
