HABITAT B
Habitat B is a speculative design-research project which focuses on extraterrestrial architecture on the moon. At its core is ROXY (Regolith to OXYgen and Metals Conversion), a groundbreaking process discovered in 2020 that effectively converts moon dust into oxygen. The project's design represents a fusion of machine and architecture, envisioning a future habitat that relies on sustainable resource utilization and opens up new possibilities for lunar exploration.
