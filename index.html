JULIUS ÜBERALL
I am working in Applied Research + Development at Foster + Partners in London. Prior to that I completed a MSc in Architectural Computation at University College London and a BA in architecture at the University of Applied Sciences Dusseldorf.

During my time in academia I was a scholarship holder at the German Academic Scholarship Foundation and worked as a student assistant for Dean Prof. Dipl. -Ing. Judith Reitz, Prof. Dipl. -Ing. Moritz Fleischmann and Prof. Dr. -Ing. Eike Musall.

From 2019 to 2022 I was Co-Founder of YŪ, which was an initiative to create personalised jewellery based on letters through a web embedded tool and additive manufacturing.

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer graphics and design.
