JULIUS ÜBERALL
I recently graduated with a MSc in Computer Graphics, Vision and Imaging from University College London (UCL) . Previously, I worked for 2 years in Applied Research + Development at Foster + Partners contributing to Cyclops, their environmental analysis software for real-time building simulations.


Prior I completed an MSc in Architectural Computation at UCL and a BA in Architecture at the University of Applied Sciences Dusseldorf.

I was a scholarship holder at the German Academic Scholarship Foundation and worked as a student assistant for Dean Prof. Dipl. -Ing. Judith Reitz, Prof. Dipl. -Ing. Moritz Fleischmann and Prof. Dr. -Ing. Eike Musall.

From 2019 to 2022 I was Co-Founder of YŪ, which was an initiative to create personalised jewellery based on a web embedded algorithm and additive manufacturing. Every word creates a unique geometry.
