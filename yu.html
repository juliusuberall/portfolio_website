YŪ
Words become jewellery.
YŪ by you was the initiative to make you design your personal piece of jewellery with the letters you enter into the algorithm.

Any given combination of letters produced a unique geometry for a ring, pendant or amulet in our web app which then could be changed in materiality and size. We designed and fabricated over 100 distinctive pieces out of metal using additive manufacturing
